Make Your Career Meaningful!

Women's Resource Center is a rewarding place to work with staff and volunteers united by a common goal to build a better world where everyone has a right to live in a violence-free home and to be treated with respect and dignity.
Case Manager | Youth Services Team

Under the coordination of the Program Manager and as part of the Youth Services team, the Case Manager will develop individual case plans for youth, perform assessments and provide referrals to in-house Women's Resource Center services, as well as external organizations and service providers to ensure that youth receive the highest quality of service and support.

Donor Membership Associate | Development Department

Reporting to the Membership Manager, the Donor Membership Associate is responsible for fundraising efforts for donors giving less than $1,500 annually. This position helps fulfill the mission of the Women's Resource Center as a member of the Donor Membership Team in conjunction with Major Gifts within the Development Department

Helpdesk Specialist | Information Technology Services

The Helpdesk Specialist is a desktop support position responsible for performing a variety of activities including but not limited to the remediation of desktop computer issues, the installation of desktop computers, and support of desktop computer users with their hardware and software needs. This position will both resolve tickets in an incident management system and work on various projects and deployments as instructed.

Law Project Attorney | Legal Services

In coordination with the Director of Legal Services, the Immigration Law Project Manager and Client Advocate, two very experienced contract attorneys, other Legal Services Department staff, and volunteer attorneys: Responsible for all aspects of a significant client caseload, including interviewing clients in Spanish and at various locations. This entails recognizing and triaging the needs of clients within the context of available resources, both within and outside the Women's Resource Center, and being responsive to community and institutional needs.

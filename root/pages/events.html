Volunteer Trainining

Januery 6, 2018

WRC will begin our volunteer training on Saturday Jan 6th – Jan 27th. This training is for those who have wanted to provide help to victims of domestic violence and sexual assault in a meaningful way.

The classes will take you through Domestic Violence 101, Crisis Intervention, Animal Abuse, Child Abuse, and more. When you have completed the training you will be asked to volunteer as part of WRC’s Hotline and Sexual Assault Advocate for at least 1 year.

It is a big commitment but also a very rewarding one. If you are interested please call 760-547-8817 and speak to Andrea our Volunteer Coordinator.

Walk Against Violence

February 1, 2018

Each Year, the members and staff walk from Seaport Village to Coronado Island and back as a way of speaking out against rape, sexual assault, and other violent acts that target women. They take a public stand against violence that occurs every day.

The best way to stop violence against women is to get men to stop inflicting it upon them. We hope that you support their efforts. We invite all to come and walk. The walk begins in the Seaport Village, 849 W Harbor Dr, San Diego, CA 92101.

Finally, there is no entry fee for our walk, but donations are encouraged and will go to Community Service Programs Sexual Assault Division.

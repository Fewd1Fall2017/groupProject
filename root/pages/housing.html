Safe and Sound

The Women’s Resource Center helpsvictims and their children rebuild violence-free lives and establish themselves as independent members of the community.

Emergency Shelter

Alternatives to Abuse is the agency’s shelter for battered men, women and their children. This facility is an emergency shelter focusing on moving the client forward into a more permanent, non-violent living situation. This 30-day facility offers a safe, calm, non-isolating environment available to single adult men and women, and adult men and women with children.

Learn More

Transitional Housing

Transitional housing offers a supportive environment where a victims can re-build their life, learn their own self-worth and develop the skills needed to be an independent contributing member of the community. Transitional housing features 21 apartments which range in size from efficiency, one-bedroom and two-bedroom units.

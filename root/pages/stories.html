Eleen's Story

2015

I reinvented myself when I decided to become a single mother. I was in an abusive marriage and chose to leave. With the unwavering support of a counselor, I learned about abuse and decided that I did not deserve to be mistreated. Also, I did not want my three sons to be victims or to perpetuate the cycle of abuse. Read more.
